Coord Account & Data Deletion

If you still have access to the Coord app, you can request account deletion in-app. If you cannot access the app, use the form below to request deletion of your Coord account and associated data.

What happens when your deletion request is completed

  • Your account login/authentication record is deleted.
  • Your user profile data is deleted.
  • Your stored push notification tokens are deleted.
  • You are removed from membership references on trips you joined but do not own.
  • If you only own solo trips, those trips and related trip data are deleted.

Important: If you still own a shared trip with other members, deletion may require manual resolution before completion so other users’ shared trip data is not disrupted.

Request deletion

Submit this form using the email address associated with your Coord account.

You can also email us directly at support@coord-app.com.

Response timeline

We aim to respond to deletion requests within 7 business days.
